ABOUT US

Welcome to Home Library, a quiet and cozy space where books and coffee come together. We are a unique combination of a café and a library, designed for people who love to read and relax at the same time.

Home Library was created to bring back the joy of traditional reading. In today’s digital world, many people spend most of their time online. Our goal is to give everyone a chance to slow down, unplug, and enjoy the simple pleasure of reading a book while sipping coffee or tea... .

Our collection includes a variety of books for all ages and interests, from classic literature to modern favorites. Whether you're a student, a book lover, or someone looking for a peaceful corner to unwind, you are welcome here.

Alongside our library, our café offers freshly brewed coffee, tea, pastries, and snacks. We want you to feel at home as you enjoy your reading or spend time with friends.

Home Library is more than just a business. It is a place for learning, sharing, and connecting through stories. Come visit us and discover a place where books and coffee make the perfect pair. Read More

INSPIRATION

The interior of Home Library is inspired by the owner’s love for Korean dramas. Many K-dramas feature calm and cozy cafés or libraries where characters read, relax, or share quiet moments over coffee. These scenes became the main inspiration for creating a space that feels peaceful, simple, and welcoming... .

The design follows a minimalist style that focuses on clean lines, soft lighting, and neutral colors. Natural wood, light tones, and simple furniture were chosen to create a space that feels open and relaxing. It was important to make Home Library a place where visitors could enjoy both comfort and focus.

Bookshelves line the walls, paired with cozy chairs and clean tables. Whether you’re reading, working, or enjoying coffee, each area is designed to help you feel at ease. The quiet and clutter-free environment lets your mind slow down and enjoy the moment.

This minimalist look reflects the values of Home Library: simplicity, mindfulness, and warmth. Inspired by the peaceful scenes in Korean dramas, the space offers a break from busy routines and encourages meaningful moments.

If you enjoy soft and cozy atmospheres like those in K-dramas, you’ll feel right at home here. Home Library invites you to relax, read, and stay as long as you like. Read More
